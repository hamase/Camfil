Introduction

At Camfil GROUP we provide a full procurement service with our on-site packing/forwarding facilities to ensure full quality control. Our privileged working relationship with companies has required us to source or manufacture a full range of items required for drilling and production operations. We are always interested in searching hard to find items, or locating new, more cost effective suppliers for routine requirements.
